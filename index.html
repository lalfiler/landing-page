Pugs are awesome

Pugs are great pets who love to play, go for walks, and chill. These dogs are friendly, family oriented, and are full of attitude!

Random pug facts

Pugs are originally from China dating back to 400 B.C.!

Pugs are heavy shedders. Don't forget a brush!

Pugs come in a variety of colors including black, fawn, and apricot!

Pugs have moles on their cheeks called "beauty spots"!

Pug is a breed of dog originally from China, with physically distinctive features of a wrinkly, short-muzzled face, and curled tail. The breed has a fine, glossy coat that comes in a variety of colors, most often fawn or black, and a compact, square body with well developed and thick muscles all over the body. -Wikipedia
